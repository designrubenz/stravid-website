Hi. My name is David Strauß.

Welcome to my personal website. I'm a developer from Salzburg, Austria. I mainly use CoffeeScript and Ruby to create useful software, in particular the Ruby on Rails and Ember.js frameworks. Besides programming I co-authored a book about HTML5 games. I'm also bootstrapping my own company edgy circle.

To find out more about me you can take a look at my profile, browse my list of projects or read some of my writings.

You can also find me on GitHub, Twitter and LinkedIn. In order to contact me write an email to mail@stravid.com.
